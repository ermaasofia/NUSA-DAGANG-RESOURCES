ABOUT US

Who We Are

NUSA DAGANG RESOURCES is a company specializing in the supply and processing of food ingredients, particularly in the categories of ground, minced, and spice-based products.

Established with the goal of simplifying food preparation processes, making them faster, more consistent, and more efficient, Nusa Dagang focuses on innovation within the food industry by offering high-quality products that are free from preservatives.

We understand the operational challenges faced by the F&B industry, central kitchens, and catering businesses. Therefore, our products are specially designed to help customers save time, reduce labor costs, and improve daily productivity.

With a strong commitment to quality and hygiene, every product is produced through carefully controlled processes to ensure freshness and a shelf life of up to 2 months when stored frozen.

MISSION

To provide high-quality, preservative-free food processing solutions that enhance efficiency and consistency for businesses in the food industry.

VISION

To become a leading and trusted food processing partner, delivering innovative solutions that support the growth of the food service industry.
